Overdrive 02E MkI

The 02E MkI is a JFET-based overdrive.
Its circuit replicates the classic tube amplifier layout - just smaller and on a 9VDC power supply.

This pedal features a treble-mid-bass fender-style tone stack, a gain control and a master volume control. A toggle switch allows to engage an input booster for low-output instruments like single-coil guitars.
